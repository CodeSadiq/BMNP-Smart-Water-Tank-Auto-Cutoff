About Us

We are a team of young innovators committed to solving everyday water management problems in Indian homes. Our goal is to simplify how people control water tank motors—saving time, energy, and resources.

BMNP started with a simple idea after seeing how often people forgot to turn off their water motors. We saw tanks overflowing, electricity bills rising, and precious water being wasted. That’s when we built a system that solves all of it—automatically.

Our Mission

Our mission is to eliminate water wastage and manual effort through a smart, mechanical solution that’s affordable and easy to install.

Our Vision

To make smart, sustainable water management accessible to every Indian home—through simple, affordable, and electricity-free automation.

Our Product

BMNP Smart Water Tank Auto Cut-Off System is a mechanical solution designed to stop your water motor automatically when your overhead tank is full. It requires no electricity, sensors, or manual monitoring. This plug-and-play device is ideal for Indian households, where overflowing tanks and energy waste are common issues.


Built from high-quality materials, the system is durable, easy to install, and works with most existing water pump setups. Whether in homes, apartments, schools, or rural water supply schemes, BMNP offers a smart, safe, and sustainable way to manage water.
